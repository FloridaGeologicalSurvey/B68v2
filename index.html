The purpose of this study is to refine the hydrogeological framework of the region to facilitate science-based decision making with regard to the protection, conservation and management of southwest Floridaâ€™s water resources.

Data from more than 1050 wells (including offshore boreholes) serve as control for these surfaces. In addition, synthetic wells were used to provide lateral (edge) control during map surface interpolation. This data was translated into threejs using the qgis2threejs plugin for QGIS.

Please note that translation and resampling errors might occur in the borehole visualization. Some wells might appear above land surface due to downsampling of the DEM to a lower resolution.

For more information, data, or publications, visit the website of the Florida Geological Survey.
